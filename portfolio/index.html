ABOUT ME

I'm a sophomore at the University of Maryland pursuing a degree in multiplatform journalism and will pursue a minor in general business.

I've worked as a freelance reporter for The Diamondback for the past two semesters and have written stories that provide UMD students with information about issues and events across campus. As a reporter, I pitch newsworthy and timely stories relevant to the University of Maryland College Park community.

I'm interested in reporting on politics and businesses to provide a public service, which I view as the most important aspect of journalism. Through my reporting, I want to hold governments and companies accountable, elevate underrepresented voices and help the public best understand the issue and decisions that impact them most.

THE EVOLUTION OF DISNEY CHANNEL

The Disney Channel impacted pop culture through colloquial phrases that defined viewers' childhoods, including "Sweet niblets!" and "What's the stitch?," as well as with songs from TV shows and movies that still find a way on many adults' playlists. The channel also turned several child stars into global icons, proving its indelible impact. This website goes through the evolution of the TV channel and the memorable figures and projects from the network.

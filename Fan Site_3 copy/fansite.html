About The Artist

Olivia Rodrigo is an American singer-songwriter and actress. She gained recognition for her music from her debut single "Drivers License," which smashed several charting and streaming records, including achieving the biggest debut in Rolling Stone 100 Songs chart history. A three-time Grammy-winning artist, Rodrigo has released two critically acclaimed albums, SOUR in 2021 and GUTS in 2023, and her 2024 Guts World Tour sold out across the world.

About the Fan

I became a fan of Olivia Rodrigo when she played Nini Salazar-Roberts on the Disney+ original series High School Musical: The Musical: The Series. I enjoyed her original song that she wrote for the show called "All I Want," and have been listening to her music since. I always thought that Rodrigo had a skill in accurately portraying teenage angst and heartbreak through her lyrics and I feel that she is one of the only artists in my generation that writes music to young adults and not just for them.
